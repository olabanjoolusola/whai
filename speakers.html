Why These Voices Matter

What the speaker lineup adds to the conference

The speaker roster is designed not only to attract interest, but to frame the conference intellectually. Each invited scholar expands the event’s ability to hold serious conversation across methods, traditions, and disciplines.

Historical Depth

The conference situates AI within longer histories of automation, reason, text, media, and institutional knowledge.

Interpretive Strength

Literary and philosophical approaches help illuminate the meanings, metaphors, and assumptions embedded in AI systems.

Ethical Reach

Questions of fairness, responsibility, power, and public consequence remain central to the conference’s broader vision.
